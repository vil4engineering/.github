# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We build a learning environment where two generations can grow as engineers — with curiosity, honesty, and respect.

## Expected behavior

- Be precise and kind.
- Prefer evidence over ego.
- Credit sources and collaborators.
- Teach when you learn something useful.
- Separate private career material from public curriculum / org material.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discrimination.
- Publishing private career, salary, or personal third-party data without consent.
- Inventing experience or fabricating Evidence.
- Deliberately low-quality drive-by changes that ignore templates and checks.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, close, or remove content that violates this code. Serious or repeated violations may lead to blocked participation.

Report concerns to the organization owner via GitHub (private contact on the org profile when available).
